Weapons

Pick your playstyle. Below are quick summaries—turn each into its own detail page later if you want.

Great Sword

Massive damage with charged slashes. Demands patience and positioning.

  • Pros: Huge burst, part breaks
  • Cons: Slow, punishable

Long Sword

Fluid counters and spirit gauge management for sustained DPS.

  • Pros: Stylish counters
  • Cons: Greed = cart

Bow

Mobile ranged pressure with coatings and stamina management.

  • Pros: Safe chip, elemental builds
  • Cons: Stamina hungry
